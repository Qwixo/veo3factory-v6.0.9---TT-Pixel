Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience and understand how our website is used.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They cannot be disabled.

  • Session cookies: Maintain your session during your visit
  • Security cookies: Protect against fraud and ensure secure connections
  • Cookie consent: Remember your cookie preferences

Duration: Session or 1 year maximum

2.2 Analytics Cookies (Optional)

These help us understand how visitors interact with our website.

  • Google Analytics: Website usage statistics (anonymized)
  • Performance tracking: Page load times, error tracking

Duration: Up to 2 years

Legal basis: Your consent

2.3 Marketing Cookies (Optional)

Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign effectiveness.

  • Conversion tracking: Measure advertising effectiveness
  • Retargeting: Show relevant ads on other websites

Duration: Up to 1 year

Legal basis: Your consent

2.4 Functional Cookies (Optional)

Enhance your experience with additional features.

  • Preferences: Remember your settings and choices
  • Chat support: Enable customer support features

Duration: Up to 1 year

Legal basis: Your consent

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics (privacy-enhanced mode)
  • Payment processors: Secure payment processing
  • Social media: Social sharing functionality

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control cookies through:

  • Cookie banner: Manage preferences when you first visit
  • Browser settings: Block or delete cookies directly
  • Opt-out tools: Third-party opt-out mechanisms

5. Browser Cookie Settings

You can control cookies in your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. Check this page periodically for updates.
